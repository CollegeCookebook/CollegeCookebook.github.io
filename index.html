Recipe of the Day:

Eel Rice: Unlike any other rice in the world for just $5!

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 380
  • Fat: 15g
  • Protein: 21g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g

    • Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 pack of 6oz frozen roasted eel
  • 1 Tbs of Unagi Sushi Sauce

    • Directions

  • 1. Wash the rice. Cook in the rice cooker with 1 cup of water till done.
  • 2. Defrost the eel , roast in the small oven with 350F for 10 minutes.
  • 3. Mix the rice with sushi sauce and transfer the eel on top of the rice. Cook in rice cooker for another 5 minutes.
